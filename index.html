About Me

Hello! I'm Marie Colleen Tingas, a passionate Computer Science student with a specialization in UI/UX Design. I combine technical expertise with creative design thinking to create digital experiences that are both functional and beautiful.

My journey in technology began with a curiosity about how things work, which led me to pursue Computer Science. Along the way, I discovered my passion for user experience design and the art of creating intuitive, user-centered digital solutions.

I believe that great design is not just about how something looks, but how it works. My goal is to bridge the gap between technology and human needs, creating solutions that are not only technically sound but also delightful to use.
